Blooming Tea

Blooming tea is a category of special Chinese tea that has been hand woven together with flowers that unbelievably "blooms" in your cup. Traditionally blooming tea is made with green tea, along with either jasmine, chrysanthemum, osmanthus, lily or amaranth flowers.
